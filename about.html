A Structured Model That Delivers Results

Grevenco operates through a bottom-up agricultural model designed to ensure effective coordination, continuous farmer support, and efficient project implementation across rural communities. This structure enables clear communication, monitoring, and accountability at every level.

  • Farmer Clusters – Organized groups that form the foundation of production and collaboration
  • Cluster Leaders – Local coordinators who oversee activities and support farmers directly
  • Agronomists – Technical experts providing guidance on soil health, crop management, and best practices
  • Regional Managers – Supervising operations, ensuring performance, and aligning projects with objectives
